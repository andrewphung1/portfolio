Smart IoT Plant Monitor

Keep Your Plants Alive with Real-Time Monitoring!

ESP32 & Embedded Systems MicroPython Sensors Integration Actuators Integration IoT Integration

Designed and built an ESP32-based IoT plant watering system that automatically irrigates a plant based on real-time soil conditions. Integrated a soil moisture sensor, DHT11 temperature/humidity sensor, and a relay-driven mini water pump so watering only triggers below a calibrated moisture threshold, with MicroPython firmware handling filtering, control logic, and safety timeouts. Over Wi-Fi, the ESP32 connects to a cloud email service to notify users when the plant is watered, and a 16√ó2 I¬≤C LCD displays live moisture, ambient conditions, and pump status as a simple ‚Äúplant health dashboard.‚Äù This project strengthened my experience with ESP32, IoT connectivity, and end-to-end sensor/actuator integration.
